+==============================================================================+
|  FULL SYSTEM DATA JOURNEY  --  ONE FAULT EVENT, STEP BY STEP                |
+==============================================================================+

  STEP  1  -->  Cell 3 voltage sensor reads 1.25V (should be 3.82V)
                |
  STEP  2  -->  ADC driver converts raw signal to digital number
                |
  STEP  3  -->  Zephyr Thread 1 converts ADC number to 1.25 Volts
                |
  STEP  4  -->  DSP filter confirms it is not a spike -- it is real
                |
  STEP  5  -->  Bridge.put("voltages", "3.82,3.81,1.25,3.83")
                |
  STEP  6  -->  Linux gateway reads bridge, builds JSON packet
                |
  STEP  7  -->  MQTT publishes JSON to Snapdragon PC over Wi-Fi
                |
  STEP  8  -->  backend.py extracts 8 values, builds [1x8] array
                |
  STEP  9  -->  Hexagon NPU runs Trust Model -- Cell 3 = 14% trust
                |
  STEP 10  -->  Hexagon NPU runs SOH Model -- Battery = 94.2% healthy
                |
  STEP 11  -->  14% < 30% threshold --> FAULT!
                |
  STEP 12  -->  Publishes "FAULT" to "ev/analytics/trust_status"
                |
  STEP 13  -->  Arduino gateway receives "FAULT"
                |
  STEP 14  -->  bridge.put("trust_status", "FAULT")
                |
  STEP 15  -->  Zephyr Thread 3 reads FAULT, flashes !! on LED matrix
                |
  STEP 16  -->  WebSocket sends alert to 3D dashboard and phone app
                |
  STEP 17  -->  Phone app sounds alarm: "Cell 3 Sensor Disconnect -- 14%"
                |
  STEP 18  -->  User asks Llama-3: "What is wrong with Cell 3?"
                |
  STEP 19  -->  Llama-3 reads 1,000 cached readings, replies offline:
                "Sensor disconnect on Cell 3. Check JST connector."
                |
  STEP 20  -->  Cloud receives incident snapshot via HTTPS (10s data)
                |
  STEP 21  -->  Cloud retrains model with new fleet fault data overnight
                |
  STEP 22  -->  AI Hub compiles new model for Hexagon NPU (QNN format)
                |
  STEP 23  -->  OTA pushes new model to all fleet cars silently
