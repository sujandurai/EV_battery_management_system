================================================================================
EV GUARDIAN AI: THE SIMPLE, LAYPERSON'S SYSTEM BLUEPRINT
================================================================================

Sometimes technical jargon like "RTOS", "NPU", "GATT", and "ONNX" gets in the 
way of understanding how the system actually works. Here is the entire system 
explained using simple real-world analogies:

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
1. THE CORE ANALOGY
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
• THE SENSORS    = The Eyes and Ears (feeling the heat, voltage, and shakes).
• THE FILTERS    = The Noise Eraser (rubbing out static/spikes from bumps).
• THE BRIDGE     = The Internal Messenger (passing data from sensors to network).
• THE GATEWAY    = The Mailbox (sending data to the PC, or caching it offline).
• THE CAR PC     = The Local Doctor (making split-second decisions inside the car).
• THE MOBILE APP = The Dashboard Light (showing dials or sounding alarms to driver).
• THE CLOUD      = The Central Hospital (collecting records to make the medicine better).

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
2. THE SIMPLE STEP-BY-STEP DATA FLOW
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

[ THE BATTERY SENSORS ]
- Continuously measures: How hot is the battery? Are the voltages normal?
  Is there gas leaking? Is the car shaking?
        │
        ▼
[ THE NOISE ERASER (DSP Filter) ]
- Keeps the system from panicking. If a bump on the road causes a tiny voltage 
  spike for a microsecond, the filter rubs it out so there is no false alarm.
        │
        ▼
[ THE INTERCOM MESSENGER (Bridge) ]
- Hands the clean, noise-free numbers over to the networking chip on the card.
        │
        ▼
[ THE MAILBOX BOARD (MPU Gateway) ]
- Wraps the numbers nicely to travel over Wi-Fi.
- If Wi-Fi is working: Mails it straight to the Car PC.
- If Wi-Fi is down: Pockets the mail in a safe storage locker (eMMC cache) 
  and mails it later when the car connects to Wi-Fi.
        │
        ▼
[ THE LOCAL DOCTOR (Snapdragon PC Engine) ]
- The brain of the car. It uses built-in smart brain hardware (Hexagon NPU).
- It analyzes the data in less than 1.2ms.
- It asks: "Is Cell 3 running low because it is broken, or just because a wire 
  vibrated loose?" 
        │
